📋 Overview

POORNIMA University ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all students, faculty, staff, applicants, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information.

By using our website, management system, mobile applications, or any POORNIMA services, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

📌 Key Principle: We collect only what we need, store it only as long as necessary, and never sell your personal data to third parties.

📦 Data We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on your relationship with POORNIMA University:

Information You Provide

  • Name, date of birth, gender, nationality
  • Email address, phone number, postal address
  • Academic qualifications and transcripts
  • Identification documents (Aadhaar, passport)
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communications sent to us

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
  • Login and logout timestamps
  • Course progress and attendance records
  • Cookies and usage tracking data

⚙️ How We Use Your Data

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Processing admission applications and enrollment
  • Providing access to the university management system
  • Managing attendance, grades, and academic records
  • Sending important academic notifications and updates
  • Processing fee payments and issuing receipts
  • Conducting examinations and issuing certificates
  • Improving our services through aggregated analytics
  • Complying with legal obligations

⚠️ Important: We do NOT use your data for marketing by third parties, nor do we use your academic records for profiling unrelated to educational purposes.

🤝 Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Government bodies — UGC, NAAC, AICTE for regulatory compliance
  • Partner institutions — for exchange programs (with your consent)
  • Payment processors — Razorpay/PayU for secure transactions only
  • Cloud providers — AWS India for secure data storage
  • Employers — verification only with explicit student consent

We never sell your personal data to advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third parties.

🔒 Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for all stored data
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication on all admin systems
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • ISO 27001 certified data centers in India
  • 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection

🛡️ In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

🍪 Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for login, session management (cannot be disabled)
  • Performance Cookies — Help us understand how users interact with our site
  • Functional Cookies — Remember your preferences (language, theme)
  • Analytics Cookies — Aggregate usage data (Google Analytics, anonymized)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

🚫
Right to Object

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@poornima.edu. We will respond within 30 days.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at privacy@poornima.edu and we will delete it promptly.

For students between 13–18 years, parental consent may be required for certain services.

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technology. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page with a new effective date
  • Send an email notification to all registered users
  • Display a prominent banner on our website for 30 days

Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
