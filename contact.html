Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I apply for admission?+
Visit our Admission page, fill the online application form, upload required documents, and pay the application fee of ₹1,500.
What scholarships are available?+
We offer Merit Excellence (100%), Need-Based (50%), and Sports/Arts (25%) scholarships. Check our Admission page for details.
Is hostel accommodation available?+
Yes, we have separate hostels for boys and girls with a capacity of 8,000 students. Air-conditioned rooms, WiFi, gym, and mess facilities included.
What is the placement record?+
98% of our students are placed within 6 months. Average package: ₹12 LPA. Highest package: ₹1.8 Cr (Google, 2024).
Can I visit the campus before applying?+
Absolutely! Campus tours are available Monday–Saturday. Book your slot via the Contact form or call our admissions office.
How do I track my application status?+
After submission, you'll receive a unique Application ID. Log into our portal using that ID to track your status in real-time.
